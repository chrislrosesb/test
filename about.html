Background

Chris Rose is Vice President of Cybersecurity and Network Operations at Aseva, a Santa Barbara–based technology services company. Aseva helps midsize organizations design, implement, and manage the technology their businesses rely on every day — helping IT leaders build reliable and secure infrastructure without having to manage dozens of vendors.

Chris has spent more than twenty years working with business leaders, CIOs, and IT teams to plan and deploy modern network and security environments. At Aseva, he evaluates new technologies, develops partnerships with vendors, and guides customers toward solutions that improve reliability, security, and performance.

At Aseva

Chris leads teams responsible for network operations, client services, and cybersecurity strategy. His focus is on helping organizations navigate an increasingly complex vendor landscape and build infrastructure that is both resilient and secure.

Aseva's approach is built around long-term partnership with its clients — not just implementing technology, but ensuring it continues to serve the business as it grows and evolves.
